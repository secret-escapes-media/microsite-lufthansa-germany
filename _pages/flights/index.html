Acquaint yourself with Undiscovered Germany and fly there in style on Germany’s biggest and most awarded airline; Lufthansa.

Proudly taking a place as one of the 10 best premier airlines in the world, Lufthansa has just been independently rated as Europe’s first and only five-star airline.

With outstanding staff making you feel comfortable and welcome on board, plus an exemplary experience both in the air and on the ground, Lufthansa is the natural choice for any discerning traveller who won’t compromise on superb quality and service.

page-nav: - id: experience title: The Experience - id: family title: Family Services key-routes: >

Lufthansa and its partners offer flights to many exciting destinations in Germany. Lufthansa Group airlines fly from 14 UK airports to Frankfurt, Munich, Cologne, Dusseldorf, Berlin and other popular German cities.

New this summer: Lufthansa is increasing the number of flights from Glasgow to Frankfurt and Munich.

If you’re flying long-haul, you can explore the globe with Lufthansa via their hubs at Frankfurt and Munich – a perfect opportunity for a pre-flight city break.

sections: - title: The Lufthansa experience id: experience features: - title: Economy id: economy link: https://www.lufthansa.com/uk/en/Economy-Class content: >

To fly economy with Lufthansa is anything but basic. On short-haul flights, the slimline seats create maximum cabin space, which means maximum legroom for you. Included snacks and drinks keep you refreshed and payable Wi-Fi is available on some flights, keeping you connected from the skies.

- title: Business id: business link: https://www.lufthansa.com/uk/en/Business-Class content: >

Go business class for a truly luxurious all-round experience. Short-haul business class flights offer a generous two hold bags per person, access to an airport lounge, priority check-in and boarding, a full meal served on fine china, plus additional personal space with a guaranteed empty middle seat in a row of three.

- title: What’s onboard? id: whats-onboard link: https://www.lufthansa.com/uk/en/flynet content: >

Fly short-haul and you can Instagram that jaw-dropping view from the plane window or send that important email with payable Lufthansa FlyNet® Wi-Fi, available on selected flights.

Flying with little ones can be a challenge – which Lufthansa are there to help you with. Rely on their tips and checklists and start your family adventure stress-free.

- title: Family Check-in id: check-in link: https://www.lufthansa.com/uk/en/Check-In-With-The-Family content: >

Discover the ease of Family Check-in at Frankfurt and Munich airports. Play areas and changing facilities make boarding your flight easy and convenient.
